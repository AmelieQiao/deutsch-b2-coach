# Study Scope

## Goal
Pass the German B2 exam with a practical and exam-oriented focus.

## Current focus
- Reading
- Writing
- Grammar
- Vocabulary related to reading and writing

## Not in scope for now
- Listening
- Speaking

## Materials to be used
- A2 past papers
- B1 past papers
- B2 past papers
- Reading materials
- Writing materials
- Grammar materials
- Vocabulary materials
- Other uploaded materials

## Evaluation dimensions
- Reading comprehension
- Vocabulary range
- Grammar accuracy
- Writing task completion
- Writing structure and coherence
- Common mistakes and weak patterns

## Working principles
- Start with a baseline level test
- Estimate current level and distance to target scope
- Identify weak areas
- Learn step by step
- Grammer knowledge breakdown and output memororizing tips in forms of tables, bullets,summaries, and diagrams
- Test weak areas frequently
- Summarize grammar rules, patterns, and memory aids
- Save all progress into records
